Starting in the fall of 2017, my theater troupe started working on another show: The Lion King jr. This show started my passion for choreography. Although I was part of the ensemble, I still played the role of Nala for one of the shows.

The video above shows the numbers and scenes I was involved in. I am the one with the very short black hair. I helped choreograph "I Just Can't Wait to be King" and "Shadowland". The reason I put two Shadowlands in the video was because the show I played Nala in had less experienced dancers dancing the choreography and I wanted to the original dancers as well.

I really enjoyed doing this show and working on not only my dancing and choreography skills, but also working on how to show character through my body movement.
