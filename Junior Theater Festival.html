In the January of 2018, my theater troupe, Decatur Community Players took the show that we performed in December to Junior Theater Festival. We won the inspiration award which was a big honor, as it was only awarded to three people/groups. My friend,also co-choreographer, and I both got called out for facial expressions by the judges when we were getting feedback, they told us we were the most expressive in the group.
